# Consolidate Docs

Promote durable truth, audit cohesion, and integrate practice learnings.

## Steps

1. Verify that relevant ADRs, architecture docs, READMEs, and plan status markers reflect current reality.
2. Extract any remaining durable conclusions from plans into permanent docs.
3. Audit cohesion across:
   - `.agent/practice-core/*`
   - `.agent/practice-index.md`
   - directives, prompts, rules, commands, and skills
4. Check the practice box:
   - `.agent/practice-core/incoming/`
   - `.agent/practice-context/incoming/`
5. Consider whether any structural learning clears the bar for Practice evolution.
6. Record what was integrated and why in the napkin.
