## Calibration notes

- **Severity inflation was common in the raw candidates.** Several "critical/high losslessness" items targeted code
  paths that are currently unreachable (R7) or correct-by-design (R1, R2). Reachability analysis (does it fire through
  the public/shipped path?) was applied to every finding, which is why the surviving Criticals were each reproduced
  end-to-end.
- **Agent disagreement was a useful signal.** The contradiction between "`complexityThreshold` is a dead option" and
  "`complexityThreshold` NaN corrupts decisions" (R8) flagged that at most one could be true; verification showed the
  former is correct and resolved the latter.
- **Duplicates were not double-counted.** The Zod-refinement no-ops (4 reports), `isRecord`-rejects-`{}` (3), and
  `maybePretty` (3) collapse into single findings (C6, C4/M3, M11). The full mapping is in `10`.
