# Metacognition

Before planning work, pause.

Think hard about what you are about to do. Those are your thoughts.
Reflect on those thoughts. Those are your reflections.
Consider what those reflections teach you about the original problem, your assumptions, and the likely failure modes. Those are your insights.

Ask:

- What changed in how I see the problem?
- Why did it change?
- Would I solve it differently now?
- What is the bridge from outcome to impact to value?

This process costs little and prevents shallow execution.
